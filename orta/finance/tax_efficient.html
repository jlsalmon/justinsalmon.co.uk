Tax Efficient

Solar PV delivers a long term solid and dependable return. As a consequence it is well suited to careful tax efficient structuring of the initial investment delivering lower up front costs and enhanced returns. Orta Solar focus on ensuring best returns from your PV investment.

Enterprise Investment Scheme

EIS was launched in 1994 as a non-aggressive tax shelter to replace the Business Expansion Scheme. In the Budget 2011 the relief was extended relief and businesses were permitted to invest. It provides an unlimited Capital Gains Tax shelter . The restrictions imposed on funds raised of £2m per company allow plenty of scope for multiple small Solar PV systems.

Orta Solar work closely with the Chancery Partnership on structuring tax efficient mechanisms for Solar PV investment. We have close relationships with several UK focused PV funds and have been integral to successfully raising and investing over £60M in UK solar PV thus far.

EIS Tax Relief - Income Tax

  • 30% Income Tax relief to £500,000 for 2011/2012
  • Conditions apply! – qualifying companies, trades, etc.
  • Relief can be claimed in tax coding; or
  • Repayment from HM Revenue & Customs

EIS Tax Relief - Capital Gains Tax

  • Deferral of CGT liability until EIS shares disposed of
  • Disposal of EIS shares exempt from CGT - subject to conditions

EIS Tax Relief - Inheritance Tax

  • Up to 100% IHT relief – BPR
  • 2 year minimum holding period applies
  • Death bed planning
