Self or Fully Funded

A carefully developed UK Solar PV investment delivers consistent long term returns comfortably over 10% IRR. Orta Solar deliver quality projects that are either fully funded by you, the client, or we finance a PV installation in return for the feed in tariff and electricity revenues. Uniquely, Orta Solar offer a tax efficient mechanism to ‘wrap’ the investment delivering significantly reduced initial investment and boosting long term returns.

Financing a 50kW PV system requires the client to rent their roof space to the financier for a 25year term. The property can be sold in this period but power production revenues cannot be stopped unless compensation payments are made for the period of move or repair to the roof.

Orta Solar deliver well developed PV projects matching them to appropriate funds. The careful use of tax breaks and funding the project through an EIS compliant scheme can boost the investment returns substantially.

As of August 2011 DECC reduced the FIT for large installations: 250kW and over is now 8p (from 29.3p!), for 150kW- 250kW it is now 15p, 50kW-150kW 19p and 50kW or less has remained at 32.9p. Returns of 10% IRR are possible still with the 50kW
